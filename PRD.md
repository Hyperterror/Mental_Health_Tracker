# PROJECT PRD

## Project Name

**MindSprint AI**

### Tagline

**Study Smarter. Stay Stronger.**

---

# 1. Problem Statement

Students preparing for high-pressure examinations such as NEET, JEE, CUET, CAT, GATE, UPSC, and Board Exams often experience significant emotional and psychological challenges throughout their academic journey.

These challenges include:

* Exam anxiety
* Burnout
* Self-doubt
* Lack of motivation
* Fear of failure
* Result-related stress
* Social comparison
* Poor study-life balance
* Sleep deprivation
* Emotional isolation

Existing productivity applications focus primarily on maximizing study hours, while mental wellness applications rarely understand the unique challenges faced by examination aspirants.

As a result, students often continue studying despite worsening stress levels, reduced productivity, and declining mental health.

There is a need for a simple, engaging, and personalized platform that helps students monitor their emotional well-being, recognize stress patterns, maintain healthy study habits, and receive timely interventions before burnout occurs.

---

# 2. Solution Statement

MindSprint AI is an AI-powered student wellness companion that combines:

* Smart Pomodoro-based study tracking
* Mood monitoring
* Stress trigger identification
* AI-powered emotional reflection
* Personalized wellness recommendations
* Gamified healthy habits

The platform helps students maintain peak performance while protecting their mental well-being.

Rather than simply encouraging students to study more, MindSprint AI promotes balanced preparation by understanding emotional patterns, detecting burnout risks, and recommending personalized recovery actions.

---

# 3. Vision

Create a future where academic success and mental well-being coexist, enabling students to achieve their goals without sacrificing their emotional health.

# 6. Key Features

## A. Smart Study Companion

### Adaptive Pomodoro Timer

Instead of fixed intervals:

System adjusts based on:

* Stress level
* Mood score
* Focus score
* Session history

Example:

| Condition       | Study Time | Break Time |
| --------------- | ---------- | ---------- |
| Relaxed         | 50 min     | 10 min     |
| Moderate Stress | 30 min     | 7 min      |
| High Stress     | 20 min     | 10 min     |

---

## B. Mood Tracking

### Daily Mood Check-in

Students select:

😊 Happy

😌 Calm

😐 Neutral

😟 Anxious

😫 Overwhelmed

Additional inputs:

* Stress level slider
* Energy level slider
* Sleep quality

Completion Time:
< 10 seconds

---

## C. Stress Trigger Tracker

Students can identify:

* Exam pressure
* Family expectations
* Social comparison
* Incomplete syllabus
* Poor mock scores
* Result anxiety
* Lack of sleep
* Time management

AI tracks recurring patterns.

Example:

"Your stress is consistently higher on days after mock tests."

---

## D. AI Reflection Journal

Students answer:

* What went well today?
* What challenged you today?
* What is one thing you'll improve tomorrow?

AI generates:

* Reflection summaries
* Emotional trends
* Wellness insights

Examples:

Suggestions:

* Eye relaxation

### Anxious Student

Suggestions:

* Breathing exercise
* Grounding technique
* Task breakdown

### Burnout Risk

* Sleep prioritization
